# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Experimental researchers and materials scientists in R&D environments. They need to analyze raw instrument signals (XRD, XPS, FTIR, Raman) and trace features to verified scientific claims under specific experimental condition locks.

## Product Purpose
An autonomous scientific workflow intelligence system that transforms experimental evidence into defensible scientific decisions through structured reasoning, validation awareness, and reproducible scientific memory. It prevents overclaiming and ensures transparent, auditable evidence-to-report workflows.

## Brand Personality
Rigorous, Trustworthy, Precise. Focuses on editorial scientific precision, high information density, clear contrast, and clear hierarchy.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS clichés: over-rounded card grids (e.g., >16px radii), tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows, and ghost cards (pairing a 1px border with a soft, wide drop shadow). It is not a generic spectra viewer, graph dashboard, or simple notebook replacement.

## Design Principles
1. Evidence before conclusions: Design components to highlight the raw data and direct observations before presenting hypotheses or conclusions.
2. Bounded scientific claims: Keep the limitations, validation gaps, and experimental conditions visible at all times.
3. Traceable reasoning paths: Make the flow from signal features to decisions inspectable and auditable.
4. Editorial density over decoration: Choose clean layout, strong typographic contrast, and intentional spacing instead of decorative shapes or background noise.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.1 AA level compliance. Text contrast ratios must meet at least 4.5:1 (including placeholder text), charts must be distinguishable without relying solely on color, and all animations must respect the `prefers-reduced-motion` media query.
